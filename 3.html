Common Plant Diseases Detected

The AI model can identify several common plant diseases and recommend preventive measures.

🍅 Tomato Leaf Blight

Symptoms

  • Brown spots on leaves
  • Yellow edges
  • Dry leaves

Cause

Fungal infection.

Prevention

  • Remove infected leaves
  • Use recommended fungicides
  • Avoid excess watering

🍃 Powdery Mildew

Symptoms

  • White powder-like coating
  • Leaf curling
  • Reduced growth

Cause

Fungus.

Prevention

  • Improve air circulation
  • Avoid overcrowding
  • Apply suitable fungicide

🌱 Bacterial Leaf Spot

Symptoms

  • Small dark spots
  • Yellow halos
  • Leaf damage

Cause

Bacteria.

Prevention

  • Use disease-free seeds
  • Avoid watering leaves
  • Remove infected plants

✅ Healthy Plant

If no disease is detected, the AI system displays that the plant is healthy along with recommendations for maintaining good plant health through proper watering, sunlight, fertilization, and regular monitoring.
