About AI Technology

Artificial Intelligence uses image recognition techniques to analyze plant leaves and identify disease patterns. The uploaded image is processed using a trained Machine Learning model, which compares it with thousands of previously learned plant images.

How AI Detects Plant Diseases

Step 1

Upload a clear image of a plant leaf.

Step 2

Image preprocessing removes noise and adjusts brightness.

Step 3

AI extracts leaf color, spots, patches, texture, shape, and disease patterns.

Step 4

The trained AI model predicts whether the plant is healthy or diseased.

Step 5

The application displays disease name, confidence score, and treatment suggestions.
