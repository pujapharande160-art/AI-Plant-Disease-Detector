AI Plant Disease Detector

Smart Farming with Artificial Intelligence

Welcome to the AI Plant Disease Detector, an intelligent web application designed to help farmers, gardeners, researchers, and agriculture students detect plant diseases quickly and accurately. Using Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), the system analyzes images of plant leaves and identifies possible diseases within seconds.

About the Project

Plant diseases are one of the major reasons for reduced crop production and financial losses in agriculture. Traditional disease diagnosis requires expert knowledge and can be time-consuming. Our AI-powered solution provides an easy and affordable way to detect diseases at an early stage, helping farmers improve crop health and productivity.
